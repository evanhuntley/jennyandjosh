Directions

, The North Carolina Aquarium at Fort Fisher

From Raleigh and points West:

  1. Take interstate 40 East
  2. After 122 miles, 40 becomes NC 132
  3. Continue on 132 through Wilmington
  4. US 421 joins 132, continue south on US 421
  5. Follow 421 through the towns of Carolina Beach and Kure Beach
  6. Turn left onto Loggerhead Road
  7. Follow Loggerhead Road directly into the Aquarium entrance

Lodging

Need a place to lay your head? We haven't set up room blocks at a local hotel just yet, but if you're the type of person who plans this far in advance, that shouldn't stop you!

Check out Priceline or Orbitz for great rates on hotels in the Carolina Beach and Kure Beach area.

Wilmington is also a short jaunt away from both locations. If you're in town for a while and want to see the area sights, a hotel in Wilmington's downtown area might be a good choice.
